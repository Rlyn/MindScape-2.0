# 🧠 MindScape — Self-Discovery Psychology Platform

> *Understand your mind. Transform your life.*

MindScape is a fully self-contained, interactive psychology quiz platform built
with **vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript** — no frameworks, no build tools,
no server required. Just open the file in a browser and start exploring yourself.

---

## ✨ Features

- **7 in-depth psychology quizzes** with 35–48 questions each (270+ total)
- **Fully personalized result profiles** with rich, detailed written analysis
- **Visual score breakdowns** — ring charts, dimension bars, spectrum charts, and trait grids
- **Expandable types explorer** — browse all possible results before you begin, with collapsible full profiles for every single outcome
- **Concrete step-by-step growth roadmaps** tailored to each individual result
- **4 theme options** — Dark Cool, Light Cool, Dark Warm, and Light Warm, persisted across sessions
- **Optional Claude AI enhancement** — paste your Anthropic API key to receive a deeply personalized AI-generated analysis on top of your results
- **Zero dependencies** — single `.html` file, works fully offline

## 🧪 The Seven Quizzes

| Quiz | Framework | Questions | What You Discover |
|---|---|---|---|
| 🧩 **Childhood Trauma** | ACE Framework | 36 | How adverse childhood experiences shaped your nervous system, emotional patterns, and core beliefs — scored across 9 adversity categories with a personalized healing roadmap |
| 💞 **Love Languages** | Chapman's 5 Languages | 40 | Your primary and secondary love languages, what fills and drains you emotionally, and how to build deeply satisfying relationships |
| 🔮 **MBTI Personality** | Myers-Briggs 16 Types | 48 | Your four cognitive dimensions and full personality type across all 16 possible profiles — with strengths, blind spots, career fits, and growth steps |
| 🌊 **Big Five OCEAN** | Big Five / OCEAN Model | 40 | Your scientifically validated trait scores across Openness, Conscientiousness, Extraversion, Agreeableness, and Neuroticism — each with level-specific growth practices |
| 💑 **Couple Compatibility** | Gottman-Informed | 35 | Your relationship's strength across five pillars: communication, values, trust, intimacy, and conflict resolution — with 5 distinct relationship profiles |
| 🌀 **Personality Archetype** | Jung's 12 Archetypes | 48 | Your dominant Jungian archetype — the universal character pattern at the core of your motivations, fears, shadow, and life story — plus your secondary archetype |
| 🔗 **Attachment Style** | Bowlby & Ainsworth | 40 | Your adult attachment pattern — Secure, Anxious, Avoidant, or Disorganized — with developmental origins, shadow patterns, compatibility insights, and a healing roadmap |

## 📊 What Each Result Includes

Every quiz result page delivers a complete, multi-section analysis:

- **Named profile** with a personalized tagline and description
- **Score visualization** — ring charts, bar charts, or spectrum displays
- **Core motivation & fear** (archetype and attachment quizzes)
- **Developmental origins** — how the pattern was shaped (attachment quiz)
- **Shadow pattern** — the unintegrated, unconscious side (archetype & attachment)
- **Strengths** — what you do naturally well
- **Blind spots** — patterns worth being aware of
- **Relationship dynamics** — how your profile shows up with others
- **Compatibility insights** — how you interact with different styles (attachment)
- **Career & life fit** — environments where you naturally thrive
- **Healing pathway** — what growth specifically looks like for your result
- **Secondary profile** — your second-highest result with explanation
- **5-step growth roadmap** — concrete, specific, and fully actionable steps
- **Affirmations** — healing and empowering statements for your journey
- **Claude AI deep-dive** — optional, deeply personalized AI-generated analysis
